Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8 eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups half-and-half
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon Vanilla Extract

Equipment:

  • Bowls
  • Measuring Cups
  • Saucepans
  • Whisk
  • Fine-mesh Sieve
  • Freezer Container

Directions:

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and add heavy cream
  2. Stir in granluated sugar and gently mix
  3. Freeze for three hours.
